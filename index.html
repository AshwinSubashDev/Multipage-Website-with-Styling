Welcome to ABC Solutions

Enhancing the College Football Experience

------

ABC Solutions is a state-of-the-art sports technology company dedicated to improving the college football game-day fan experience. We 

bring the latest and greatest technologies available to the football field, from cutting-edge digital tools to real-time data analytics, helping 

fans stay connected, engaged, and informed. Our goal is to improve every week of the football season by making it easier than ever to 

join in on the excitement every Saturday and creating lifelong memories. 

Our Core Values

  • Accessibility - Giving fans easy-to-use tools and platforms that allow them to have the in-stadium experience from anywhere makes game day easy and enjoyable for everyone.
  • Bold Innovation - Pushing the limits of technology and developing innovative digital approaches to provide college football fans with unique and compelling experiences.
  • Community - Building a stronger, more connected fan base on every campus around the country and fostering a sense of unity among fans, teams, and the beautiful game we love
